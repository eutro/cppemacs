◆ CPPEMACS_ENABLE_EXCEPTION_BOXING

#define CPPEMACS_ENABLE_EXCEPTION_BOXING   0

Define as 1 before including <cppemacs/core.hpp> to more tightly integrate Emacs signals with C++ exceptions, at the cost of performance.

Specifically, unless overridden with their Unbox or Box template parameters:

  • envw::maybe_non_local_exit() will rethrow the non-local exit, so it can be caught as cppemacs::signalled or cppemacs::thrown, and without leaving the non-local exit pending. Most parts of cppemacs that fail using exceptions use maybe_non_local_exit() to raise the error.
  • envw::run_catching() stores C++ exceptions as user-ptrs of std::exception_ptrs, and envw::rethrow_non_local_exit() unboxes them.
